NOTE: Internet connection is required for map overlays!
Instructions
Input a number of drone waypoints (red) and click 'Locate Waypoint Drones!' to place them randomly on the map.


Waypoint Drones:
Then drag and drop the mission drone (green) on the map at different locations.


Mission Drone (Drag/Drop)

The colored lines drawn between the mission drone and the waypoint drones indicate a somewhat arbitrary metric of relative distances between the mission and waypoint drones to the SkyWard IO HQ, where red is high priority and green is low priority for the mission drone to reach the waypoint drone before heading back to HQ.

You can drag the green mission drone around the map to see different priorities assigned to the waypoints.

This type of visualization could be used to indicate prioritized waypoint navigation or show remaining battery life for a drone navigating through waypoints, for example.
